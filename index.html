Introduction & Background

The Problem We're Solving

Understanding the laundry landscape for urban residents and why a dedicated platform is needed.

Why PureLoop?

The objective of this project is to create a mobile application that bridges the gap between residents or tenants of hostels, PGs (Paying Guests), and flats in Tier 1 and 2 cities with reliable laundry and ironing service providers.

The primary aim is to offer an efficient and user-friendly platform that tackles the key challenges faced by residents — from finding trustworthy providers to scheduling pickups and tracking orders seamlessly.

A case study is a detailed analysis of a design project that showcases the problem-solving process, design decisions, and user-centered approach used to create an effective and user-friendly interface. It provides valuable insights into the designer's methodology and the impact of their work.

Objectives & Goals

What We Aim to Achieve

Clear goals driving our design decisions — from user research to final prototype.

01

Seamless Service Discovery

Design an intuitive browsing experience where users can quickly find, compare, and select laundry providers based on location, services, ratings, and pricing.

02

Effortless Booking Flow

Create a streamlined booking and scheduling system with pickup/delivery date-time selection, address management, and service customization.

03

Transparent Pricing Model

Display clear per-kg pricing for washing, ironing, and bundled services so users can make informed decisions without hidden costs.

04

Real-time Order Tracking

Implement a visual order status tracker showing every stage — pickup, washing, steam ironing, out for delivery, and delivered.

05

Trust Through Reviews

Build a feedback and rating system that helps users evaluate providers and continuously improves service quality.

06

Multi-Payment Integration

Support GPay, PhonePe, Amazon Pay UPI, Credit/Debit cards, Netbanking, and Cash on Delivery for maximum convenience.

User Journey

The Complete User Flow

A visual walkthrough of the end-to-end user journey — from opening the app to completing an order.

From Discovery to Delivery

The user flow is designed to minimize friction at every step. Users open the app, sign up or log in, and are immediately presented with available services — Washing, Steam Ironing, and Dry Cleaning.

After selecting a service and provider, users schedule a pickup, choose their delivery preferences, and pay through their preferred method. Real-time order tracking keeps them informed at every stage.

Key Flow Steps:
Splash → Welcome/Onboard → Login/Sign Up → Home Screen → Service Provider → Pricing → Booking & Scheduling → Payment → Confirmation → Order Tracking → Review

Inclusive Design

WCAG Accessibility Goals

Ensuring PureLoop is usable by everyone, regardless of ability or context.

👁️
Color Contrast (AA)
Adjusting primary and secondary text against backgrounds to ensure a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for improved readability.
🗣️
Screen Reader Support
Ensuring screen readers can clearly navigate every interactive element — buttons, inputs, and status trackers — through proper labeling and structure.
⌨️
Keyboard Navigation
Ensuring focus states are clearly visible and all core flows (booking, payment) can be completed entirely via keyboard.
